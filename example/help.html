How do I remove the Coupon Printer?
 Windows Removal Instructions

Step 1:
Click the "Start" menu button and select "All Programs"

Step 2:
Click the "Coupons" program folder and select "Uninstall Coupon Printer for Windows"


This will initiate the Coupon Printer uninstaller:

The Coupon Printer will now uninstall itself, which may take a moment or two.

You should then see the following screen:

Step 3:
Click "Finish" and you are done!


 Mac OS X Removal Instructions

Step 1:
Click on the 'Finder' icon to open the Finder window.

Step 2:
Select your Macintosh local hard drive from the Finder window to display the drive's contents.

Step 3:
Locate and open the 'Applications' folder.

Step 4:
Locate and open the 'Coupons' folder from within Applications.

Step 5:
Run the 'Uninstall Coupon Printing Software' file to initiate the uninstaller.
